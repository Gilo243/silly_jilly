Services

Welcome to Silly Jilly's.

Silly Jilly's is an experienced, professional dog walking service based in Tonbridge. We work extremely hard to provide you with the safe knowledge your much loved dogs are being exercised and cared for during walks and their welfare is our highest priority. All the dogs are treated as if they are our own and we make sure they receive the exercise, stimulation and love that they need.

  • Services include dog walking, doggy daycare & puppy visits for our youngest members.
  • Super flexible service, guaranteed to take the stress out of dog ownership.
  • Local experienced dog walkers to take care of your dogs daily needs.
  • Fully insured, licensed and police checked for your peace of mind.
Funny Dogs!

Dog Walking

A walk to your dog is an essential part of their day, but with today’s hectic pace of life it can be difficult to fit the daily walk into your routine – don’t worry, we are here to help. Silly Jilly's provides the stimulation that your dog requires to be a well rounded, socialised & happy member of doggy society.

Doggy Daycare

If you are away from your pooch for extra long hours then doggy daycare may be a better option for your dog. Spending either a whole or half day with their walker, not only does your dog benefit from the company of others and social interation for the day but is also dropped home tired but happy!

Puppy Visits

When puppies are very young they require a slightly different service from our regular walks. Usually starting off with one or two ‘puppy visits’ a day to feed, cuddle and clean up after puppy, this gives us a great chance to start forming a bond before integrating puppy into a regular scheduled walk.
